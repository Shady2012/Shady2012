# Hi there, I'm Shadrack O. Aboagye 👋

**PhD Researcher | Biomedical Engineering | Wearable Electrical Impedance Tomography (EIT)**

I am a researcher specializing in the intersection of smart textiles, precision analog hardware, and computational imaging. My doctoral research focuses on developing accessible, wearable EIT systems (The "Smart Bra") for the early detection of breast cancer. 

I bridge the gap between physical sensor design and inverse problem algorithms, translating complex physiological signals into actionable clinical data.

---

### 🔬 Current Research & Expertise
* **Hardware Engineering:** Designing modular, high-fidelity PCB stacks for Data Acquisition (Signal Managers), including custom Voltage-Controlled Current Sources (Howland Pumps) and Adaptive Gain Control systems.
* **Smart Textiles:** Fabricating and characterizing skin-safe, dry-contact electrodes (Carbon-Silicone composites, Embroidered Silver Yarn) for wearable bioimpedance.
* **Computational Imaging:** Implementing Gauss-Newton solvers and custom regularizations (NOSER, Laplacian) within the EIDORS framework to reconstruct 3D conductivity maps.
* **Clinical Translation:** Bridging bench-top phantom validation with clinical feasibility protocols and regulatory compliance (IEC 60601-1).
